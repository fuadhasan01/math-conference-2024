Schedule

Conference Overview

Our conference spans three days, featuring engaging talks, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. Below is the detailed schedule for each day.

Day 1: Algebra & Number Theory

This day focuses on foundational concepts in algebra and number theory, featuring renowned speakers from around the world.

  • 09:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Opening Keynote: "The Beauty of Algebra" by Dr. Jane Doe
  • 10:15 AM - 11:15 AM: Session 1: "Modern Approaches to Number Theory" by Prof. John Smith
  • 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Workshop: "Hands-on Algebra: Solving Complex Problems" by Dr. Emily Johnson
  • 01:30 PM - 02:30 PM: Panel Discussion: "Future Trends in Algebra" with various speakers
  • 02:45 PM - 03:45 PM: Session 2: "Algebraic Structures in Cryptography" by Dr. Alice Brown
  • 04:00 PM - 05:00 PM: Networking Hour: Meet the Speakers and Attendees

Day 2: Calculus & Differential Equations

Day two dives into calculus and differential equations, emphasizing their applications in real-world problems.

  • 09:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Keynote: "Calculus in Modern Science" by Dr. Robert Green
  • 10:15 AM - 11:15 AM: Session 1: "Numerical Methods for Differential Equations" by Dr. Clara White
  • 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Workshop: "Real-World Applications of Calculus" by Dr. Max Blue
  • 01:30 PM - 02:30 PM: Session 2: "Partial Differential Equations and Their Applications" by Dr. Laura Black
  • 02:45 PM - 03:45 PM: Case Study Presentation: "Differential Equations in Biology" by Prof. Sarah Green
  • 04:00 PM - 05:00 PM: Open Forum: Discuss the Future of Calculus Research

Day 3: Applied Mathematics & Computational Methods

The final day centers on applied mathematics and computational methods, showcasing innovative solutions to complex problems.

  • 09:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Keynote: "The Role of Applied Mathematics in Industry" by Dr. Steven Grey
  • 10:15 AM - 11:15 AM: Session 1: "Mathematical Modelling in Engineering" by Dr. Helen Red
  • 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Workshop: "Using MATLAB for Mathematical Problems" by Dr. Greg Yellow
  • 01:30 PM - 02:30 PM: Session 2: "Computational Mathematics: The Future of Research" by Dr. Nancy Purple
  • 02:45 PM - 03:45 PM: Interactive Q&A: "Career Opportunities in Applied Mathematics" with industry experts
  • 04:00 PM - 05:00 PM: Closing Ceremony and Networking Reception
